Dual-Zone Time Mapper

One shared instant rendered through two time zones. Outer ring is the primary zone. Inner ring is the secondary zone. Open the menu to change zones, set hypothetical times, and step through dates and times.

Primary

Secondary

Internal clock engine uses one absolute instant and converts it into each zone. That keeps both rings synchronized during live updates, manual edits, and both date and time stepping. Day-state labels show whether the secondary zone is on the previous day, same day, or next day relative to the primary zone for the current shared instant.
